📍 Best Time to Visit Leh Ladakh:

April to June and September to October is the best time to enjoy the exotic Leh Ladakh Tour.Weather conditions changed according to the months. At the start of April, snow started melting, so the market, guest houses, hotels get ready to welcome the tourists. It is the starting time of the tourist season. Monuments are also open to visitors.

After June, at the start of Monsoon, this area has heavy rainfall as a result of it, there would be road blockages, and the condition of roads becomes worst for travelling, however, initially, Manali highway road remains in running condition along Rohtang pass. So the monsoon season is not the right time to do travelling because it is hazardous.

As the Monsoon ends, tourist activities start again with a clear sky and picturesque views. The second tourist season remains until October end after this extreme cold covers the whole area. When snowfall starts, life remains at halt due to heavy snow and minus degree temperature where locals can only survive. It continues until the end of the march. Then again, the tourist season starts at the beginning of April month.
